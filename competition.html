PITCH THE GLITCH


Ever wondered the mechanism of a successful marketing campaign? Envision presents to you it's first ever marketing competition -"PITCH THE GLITCH". Back up your marketing skills and join us for this thriller opportunity on 11th November at 7: 30 p.m.

The event will be conducted in 2 rounds. Each team will comprise of 3 members which are in the role of marketing head, sponsorship head and P.R. head.

(a) Round 1- Each team will be asked to come up with the name, tagline and pitch on a particular category of product for which the theme will be announced on the spot. The team winners from round 1 will move on to the second round but the link will be provided to anyone who wishes to watch the second round.
(b) Round 2- The winners from the first round will again be required to come up with the name, tagline and pitch on a given theme which will be announced on the spot.

Incentives:-
University certified Certificate of participation will be given to every participant who will attend both days of the marketing workshop. Winners will be receiving exciting cash rewards.
